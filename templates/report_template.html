Risk Scoring Methodology
  • Critical (9-10): Owner/Administrator roles with full control
  • High (7-8): Contributor roles with write access
  • Medium (4-6): Specific resource access (Key Vault, Storage)
  • Low (1-3): Read-only and monitoring access

Note: Risk scores are calculated based on role privileges, scope of access, and resource sensitivity.

High-Risk Role Analysis

Critical Roles Detected

The following roles pose the highest security risk and should be regularly audited:

  • Owner: Full control over resources and ability to assign roles to others
  • Contributor: Can create, modify, and delete resources but cannot grant access
  • User Access Administrator: Can assign roles to other users
  • Key Vault Administrator: Full access to secrets, keys, and certificates
  • Storage Account Key Operator: Can regenerate storage account access keys
